National Science Foundation

May 2023 – July 2023

This project focused on the use of off-the-shelf hardware to test the security of UAVs. I used a simple software defined radio (SDR) with a crystal oscillator (for ppm accuracy) and ran open-source software on the device. This was used offensively against a civilian UAV in a controlled environment. The research project proved our ability to send out fake GPS signals to the drone and the mobile device controlling it.
